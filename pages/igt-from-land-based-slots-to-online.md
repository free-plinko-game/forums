## IGT: From Land-Based Slots to Online

International Game Technology (IGT) is a global leader in the gaming industry, renowned for its extensive history in the development of gaming technologies. Founded in 1975, IGT initially made a name for itself through the manufacture of land-based slot machines before expanding into the realm of online gaming.

### Early History

IGT was originally founded as A-1 Supply by William Redd in 1975, focusing primarily on slots for land-based casinos. Throughout the late 20th century, the company established itself as an innovator by introducing several groundbreaking technologies that revolutionized the gaming landscape.

### Innovations in Land-Based Gaming

#### Video Slots

One of IGT's most significant contributions to the land-based gaming industry was the development of video slot machines. This innovation led to more dynamic gameplay and laid the groundwork for future advancements.

#### Progressive Jackpots

Another transformative innovation was the introduction of wide area progressive jackpots. In 1986, IGT launched the "Megabucks" system, which linked machines across multiple casinos, thus significantly increasing jackpot sizes. This concept of pooled progressive jackpots has become a staple in both land-based and online gaming.

### Expansion into Online Gaming

With the rise of digital technology and the internet, IGT expanded its operations to include online gaming. The move was aimed at capturing the growing market of online casino players who favored the convenience and accessibility of digital platforms.

#### Acquisition of WagerWorks

A pivotal moment in IGT's online strategy was the acquisition of WagerWorks in 2005. WagerWorks, a developer of online casino products, gave IGT an immediate entry into the online gaming market. Through this acquisition, IGT enhanced its capabilities to offer popular land-based games in digital formats.

### Development of Online Slots

IGT capitalized on its existing pool of successful land-based titles and transitioned them into the online sphere. This approach allowed IGT to leverage its well-known brand and attract players familiar with its land-based slots.

Popular games such as "Cleopatra", "Da Vinci Diamonds", and "Wolf Run" were reimagined for online platforms. These games maintain the engaging features of their physical counterparts while offering the additional advantages of interactive and mobile-friendly interfaces.

### Partnerships and Licensing

To further cement its position in online gaming, IGT has frequently engaged in partnerships and licensing agreements. These collaborations allow for the development of branded games, drawing from popular media franchises. Notable examples include titles like "Wheel of Fortune" and "Monopoly", which have been adapted both for land-based and online casino environments.

### Current Position in the Industry

Today, IGT remains a prominent force in both land-based and online gaming sectors. The company continues to innovate by adopting new technologies, such as mobile and social gaming platforms, and by exploring emerging markets worldwide.

#### Social and Mobile Gaming

IGT has embraced the growing trend of mobile gaming by ensuring that its products are optimized for smartphones and tablets. The company also participates actively in the social gaming sphere, providing free-to-play casino games that mirror their real-money counterparts.

### Impact on the Gaming Industry

IGT's transition from land-based slots to online gaming reflects the broader evolution of the gaming industry. By successfully navigating these changes, IGT has not only preserved its influence but also set standards that many other gaming companies strive to emulate.
