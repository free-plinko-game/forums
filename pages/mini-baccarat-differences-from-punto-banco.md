### Gameplay

The basic gameplay mechanics of Mini Baccarat and Punto Banco are identical. Both games are played using a standard deck of 52 cards, and the objective is to predict whether the player's hand or the banker's hand will have a total value closest to nine. Players can also bet on a third option, a tie, which occurs if both hands have the same value.
